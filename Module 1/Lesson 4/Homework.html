The Powerpuff girls

The Powerpuff girls is a series on Cartoon network created by Craig McCraken. It consists of 3 girls with the names Blossom, Buttercup and Bubbles who protect Townsville from villains.They were created by Professor Utonium's accidental mixing of ingredients and chemicals. The reason why this cartoon is my favorite is because the unity and strength of the girls while fighting off the villains is impeccable. This cartoon highlights the sides of female friendship and it demonstrates how good always wins agains evil.
